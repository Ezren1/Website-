Hadith Contradicts Quran: Direct Clashes

Hadith Contradicts Hadith: Internal Inconsistencies

Discover how different Hadith narratives often contradict each other, undermining their reliability as a secondary source of law.

Creation of Adam

Some Hadith state Adam was created on Friday, others on Saturday or Sunday. These conflicting narratives highlight issues in preservation.

Number of Prayers

While the Qur'an specifies prayer times, some Hadith describe variations in the number of daily prayers, even after Prophet Muhammad's migration to Medina.

Prophet's Diet

Hadith offer conflicting accounts of the Prophet's dietary habits, including consumption of certain foods which are then debated by scholars based on other Hadith.

Hadith Attack Prophet's Character

Certain Hadith depict the Prophet forgetting parts of his prayer, which is inconsistent with the Qur'an's description of his guidance and meticulousness.

A few Hadith narratives contain stories that could be interpreted as moral lapses or questionable actions, which are strongly rebutted by the Qur'anic depiction of his exalted character (68:4).
